About Us

The Cal Poly Billiards Club is a student-run organization at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. We provide a space for Cal Poly students to improve and play billiards games, such as 8-ball, 9-ball, and 10-ball pool. No experience required to join, welcoming all casual, beginner, and competitive players.

Events

The Cal Poly Billiards Club meets once a week at the Mustang Langs in the University Union from 6-9 PM. Join us for casual games and food free of charge! No prior experience required to play!

Stay tuned for tournaments later this year at Hot Shots Billiards in Pismo Beach!
